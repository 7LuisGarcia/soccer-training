Season Planner

Organize your soccer season from preseason preparation to final matches. Build a clear plan for training, games, player development, and team goals.

Season Planning Steps

1. Set Team Goals

Decide what your team wants to achieve this season, such as improving fitness, building teamwork, developing technical skills, or competing for a championship.

2. Build a Training Calendar

Schedule training sessions, match days, recovery days, tournaments, and team meetings so players and coaches know what to expect.

3. Plan Training Phases

Divide the season into preseason, early season, midseason, and postseason phases. Each phase should focus on different priorities.

4. Track Player Development

Monitor attendance, performance, fitness, technical skills, and personal goals to help each player improve throughout the season.

Season Phases

  • Preseason: Build fitness, team chemistry, and basic tactics.
  • Early Season: Establish formations, roles, and match routines.
  • Midseason: Adjust tactics, review performance, and prevent burnout.
  • Late Season: Sharpen match preparation and focus on key games.
  • Postseason: Review progress and set goals for next season.

What to Include in Your Plan

Training Focus

Choose weekly themes such as passing, pressing, finishing, defending, possession, transitions, or set pieces.

Match Schedule

Keep track of opponents, match locations, kickoff times, travel plans, and preparation notes.

Player Rotation

Plan playing time, substitutions, position changes, and opportunities for player growth.

Recovery & Rest

Add rest days and lighter sessions to reduce fatigue and lower the risk of injury.

Example Weekly Plan

Day Focus Example Activity
Monday Recovery Light stretching, mobility, and match review
Tuesday Technical Training Passing patterns, first touch, and possession drills
Wednesday Tactical Work Team shape, pressing, and defensive organization
Thursday Game Preparation Set pieces, finishing, and opponent-specific tactics
Friday Light Session Short-sided games and confidence-building activities
Saturday Match Day Warm-up, team talk, match, and post-game notes

Coaching Tips

  • Plan ahead, but stay flexible when games, weather, or player availability changes.
  • Balance hard training days with recovery days.
  • Use player feedback to adjust the season plan.
  • Review progress every few weeks and update team goals.
  • Connect every training session to a larger season objective.
